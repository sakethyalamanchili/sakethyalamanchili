### 👨‍💻 About Me

> _"I debug for fun. Coding is just how I get there."_

I am a **Master’s Candidate in Data Science & Analytics** at [Florida Atlantic University](https://www.fau.edu/), specializing in **Reinforcement Learning**, **AI**, and **Machine Learning**. 

Currently, I serve as a **Graduate Research Assistant** for the **Vertically Integrated Projects (VIP)** program, where I bridge the gap between academic research and scalable education frameworks. My background spans from rigorous IoT engineering to creative AR development, giving me a unique perspective on building systems that are both intelligent and user-centric.

* 🔭 **Current Focus:** Heterogeneous Transfer Learning & Multi-Agent Systems.
* 🎓 **Research:** Launching and mentoring AI/Data Science research tracks at FAU.
* 💼 **History:** Former Software Developer @ Levicent | AR Lens Creator (700K+ Followers).
* 🌱 **Philosophy:** Build systems that think, visualize insights that speak.
